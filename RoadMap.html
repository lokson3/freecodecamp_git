PHASE 04
OSCP Preparation — The Endgame
6–12 months of serious prep
[!]
OSCP reality check: The exam is 24 hours of hacking + 24 hours to write a report. You need to compromise 3 standalone machines + an Active Directory set (70+ points to pass). People fail multiple times. Budget $1,499 minimum. Do not attempt until you can consistently root HTB Medium machines unassisted.
TCM Security (PNPT Track)
PAID
Heath Adams' courses (Practical Ethical Hacking, Linux Priv Esc, Windows Priv Esc, Movement/Pivoting/Persistence) are the single best OSCP prep package outside of PEN-200. ~$30 per course on TCM's site. PNPT cert ($399) is a respected alternative to OSCP.
ADprivescpivotingoscp prep
→ Best value OSCP prep content
OffSec PEN-200 (PWK)
PAID
The official OSCP course. $1,499 includes 90 days lab access + 1 exam attempt. Dense PDF + video + 70+ lab machines. The lab is where you actually learn. Read everything, take notes, practice report writing from day one.
officiallabreport writing
HTB Pro Labs (RastaLabs / Offshore)
PAID
Simulated enterprise environments with full Active Directory. Essential for understanding real-world attack chains. RastaLabs and Offshore are OSCP-adjacent difficulty.
active directoryenterprise sim
0xdf / IppSec Writeups (YouTube)
FREE
IppSec's HTB machine walkthroughs are required watching. Don't watch passively — pause, try yourself first, then compare approach. 0xdf's blog writeups are equally valuable for reading methodology.
